About Us

Who we are

XYZ is a dynamic payment fintech specializing in recurring payment collections. Founded in 2020, we are committed to empowering businesses whose revenues depend on recurring payments—such as subscriptions, loan EMIs, fee collections, and rentals—by offering comprehensive, end-to-end payment solutions.

Our Unique Value Proposition

apex delivers solutions that cover the full width and depth of recurring payments, making us the ideal partner for businesses with predictable income streams.

Our key strengths include:

Speed & Agility

We swiftly build and deploy customized solutions that cater to the unique needs of each business.

Innovation

Our solutions are designed to enhance operational efficiency, streamline processes, and maximize revenue generation.

Security & Compliance

We prioritize safety and security, ensuring full compliance with relevant regulations and building trust with businesses and their customers.

Our Philosophy

At apex, Our philosophy is simple

"We promise small, deliver beyond."

We believe in making focused commitments and consistently exceeding expectations to build trust and foster long-term partnerships.

Territory Focus

Our core markets are India and the GCC countries. These regions present rich opportunities for recurring payments, and our platform is tailored to meet the specific regulatory and operational demands of these territories.

Proven Expertise

apex is a trusted and empanelled vendor with the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) for offering recurring payment solutions through the NACH platform. This allows us to provide cutting-edge services to banks and businesses across India, streamlining recurring payment processes at scale.

Operational Footprint

24 banks and 50+ NBFCs across India and GCC rely on our recurring payment collection platform. We have established sponsor bank relationships with 4 banks, ensuring robust and reliable payment processing capabilities for our clients.

Experienced Team

Our team brings over 200 person-years of banking technology experience, making us a trusted leader in the payment’s domain. We combine deep industry knowledge with fintech innovation to deliver exceptional solutions that help businesses optimize their recurring payment streams. At apex, we don’t just promise; we deliver. With a proven track record and strong partnerships, we are committed to driving your business success.
