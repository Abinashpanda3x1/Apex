Paycorp Smart Mandate

Our Smart Mandate simplifies recurring payment collection for corporates with an intuitive self-service portal and seamless NACH Mandate management.

Effortless NACH Mandate Creation

Simplify the mandate creation process for your customers with PAN & Customer ID authentication, now available alongside existing options like debit cards, net banking, and Aadhaar authentication. Paycorp ensures a smooth, secure setup process that saves time and builds trust with your customers.

  • Quick and secure mandate setup
  • Multiple options for customer convenience
  • Reduces delays and enhances experience

Processing Volumes

We process an impressive average of 1 million+ mandates and over 120 million ACH transactions each month. With peak handling capabilities of 8 million ACH debit presentations in a single day, our robust processing capacity ensures timely and accurate payments for NBFCs and their customers.

Multiple Bank Benefits for NBFCs

With Paycorp’s Multi-Bank Relationship Management, you can manage transactions and relationships across multiple banks. We simplify how you can handle financial needs, improving efficiency and security.

Collections of all EMIs

You can consolidate all your EMIs in one place, making it simpler to track and manage payments, reducing the risk of missed or late payments. Paycorp can also check customer balances before presentation and representation, ensuring successful payments with the support of account aggregator services.

Account Aggregator Integration

The Account Aggregator (AA) collects their digital financial data from one or more accounts so that you can make better decisions when it comes to choosing customers. This is done by integrating APIs with Account Aggregators.

Automated End-to-End NACH Payments collections with Self Service Portal

Effortlessly manage end-to-end NACH payments with our Self-Service Portal, designed for seamless, automated collections. Empower customers to initiate and monitor mandates independently, enhancing efficiency and control.
